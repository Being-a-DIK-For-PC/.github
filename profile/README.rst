Being a DIK
-----------

Engaging and choice-driven adult visual novel featuring college life, fraternity drama, romance, comedy, and a deeply interactive story across a university campus.

📖 Overview
-----------
Being a DIK is a narrative-driven life simulation and visual novel developed and published by PACKAGED Studios. Players take control of a young man from a low-income background who leaves his single father to attend university.

To survive the social pressures of college life, he joins the Delta Iota Kappa (DIK) fraternity. Each semester forces players to balance their academic studies, handle intense rivalry with competing fraternities, and navigate complex romantic relationships with an array of memorable characters.

Designed with cinematic rendering, branching choice mechanics, and multiple mini-games, Being a DIK focuses heavily on player agency. Your decisions directly affect your character's personality, shifting your alignment score along a spectrum from a kind, respectful student ("Chick") to an aggressive, confident bad boy ("DIK"). With its dramatic plotlines, adult humor, and deep progression paths, the game offers a highly immersive look into fictional American college culture.

🎮 Gameplay
------------
Being a DIK blends point-and-click exploration, text-based visual novel choices, and specialized mini-games. Players spend their days attending university classes, earning money through campus jobs, talking with classmates to unlock new dialogue options, and making critical decisions that permanently alter the narrative trajectory of the semester.

🌍 Explore University Grounds
-----------------------------
Each episode expands the accessible areas of the town and campus, taking players from the local student dorms and academic lecture halls to high-end fraternity mansions, local bars, and hidden off-campus locations. Every venue introduces fresh character interactions, secret plot progression paths, and timed events that keep the student life simulation constantly moving forward.
